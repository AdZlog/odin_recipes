Lasagna

Classic lasagna starts with preparing your key components: a rich meat sauce, perfectly cooked lasagna noodles, and a creamy ricotta mixture.

  • Meat Sauce
  • Cheese Mixture
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  1. Brown the ground meat and sausage with onions and garlic. Drain off excess fat. Add tomato products, herbs, and seasonings, then simmer to thicken
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions
  3. Combine ricotta, Parmesan, eggs, and seasonings in a bowl
  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    1. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    2. Layer noodles, then spread with ricotta mixture.
    3. Add more meat sauce, then mozzarella cheese.
    4. Repeat layers (noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, mozzarella) until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 30 minutes. Then, remove foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown
  6. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
