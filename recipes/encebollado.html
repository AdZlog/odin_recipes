Encebollado Recipe

Encebollado

Traditional Ecuadorian fish stew, known for its hearty broth, flavorful vegetables, and chunks of fish, particularly tuna

Ingredients

  • Albacore Tuna
  • Yuca
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Cilantro
  • Garlic, minced or crushed
  • Green pepper, chopped
  • Cumin, ground
  • Achiote
  • Water or fish stock
  • Lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For serving: Chifles, tostado corn nuts, avocado slices, and extra lime slices.

Steps

  1. Saut√© diced onion, tomato, cumin, chili powder (or achiote), and salt in oil.
  2. Add water and cilantro sprigs to the refrito and bring to a boil
  3. Cook the tuna chunks in the boiling broth for about 10-15 minutes. Remove the fish, reserving the broth, and break the tuna into pieces
  4. Cook the yuca chunks in the tuna broth until tender. Note that yuca may have fibrous threads that should be removed
  5. Combine sliced red onions, sliced tomatoes, chopped cilantro, oil, lemon or lime juice, and salt and pepper. Marinate for at least 30 minutes to 1 hour
  6. Add the cooked yuca and tuna back into the broth. Warm if necessary and adjust seasonings. Serve hot, topped with the pickled onion and tomato salsa. Serve with sides like chifles, tostado corn nuts, avocado, and lime
